Introducing YMC Earth – our conscious core collection, a refined edit of our favourite styles from over the years, sustainably constructed from natural, organic and/or recycled materials. Produced in collaboration with a selection of knitters, mills, factories and laundries who specialise in environmentally responsible manufacturing methods and social accountability. This collection represents the next step in YMC’s ongoing commitment to incorporating sustainability.
